COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT (EXCERPT)
Landlord: Empire Holdings LLC
Tenant: The Brooklyn Beanery (Coffee Shop)

Section 4.1 - Permitted Use: 
The Tenant shall use the Premises solely for the operation of a retail coffee shop, bakery, and cafe. The Premises shall not be used for any illegal purposes, nor for any heavy cooking requiring a commercial grease trap (e.g., frying, grilling of raw meats).

Section 4.2 - Quiet Enjoyment:
Landlord covenants that Tenant, upon paying the rent and performing its obligations, shall peacefully and quietly have, hold, and enjoy the Premises.

Section 8.1 - Assignment and Subletting:
Tenant shall not assign this Lease or sublet any portion of the Premises without the prior written consent of the Landlord, which consent shall not be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed.
