Deposition Transcript Excerpt: Plaintiff Sarah Jenkins
Date of Incident: October 12th, 2025
Location: SuperMart Supermarket, Brooklyn, NY

Q: Ms. Jenkins, can you describe what happened when you entered Aisle 4?
A: I was walking toward the dairy section. Suddenly, my feet went out from under me. I fell hard on my right knee.
Q: Did you see what you slipped on?
A: Yes, after I fell, I saw it was a large puddle of melted black cherry ice cream. 
Q: Did the ice cream look fresh?
A: No. It was completely melted into a sticky liquid. Furthermore, there were three distinct sets of shopping cart tracks rolled right through the middle of the puddle, and a footprint that wasn't mine. 
Q: Was there an employee nearby?
A: Yes, a young man was restocking cereal at the very end of the aisle. He had his back turned and was wearing headphones.
